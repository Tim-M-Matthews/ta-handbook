This is the **Triangle ACT staff handbook** prototype.

- Add markdown files under `content/pages` (nested folders become URL paths).
- Use frontmatter `category` to group pages on the home screen.
- Use `roles` to restrict who can see a page (see the Clinical and Operations examples).

Anyone with a mapped Google account can read pages that do not list `roles`, or that include one of their roles. Users with the `admin` role can read everything.
