## When to submit

Submit requests **at least two weeks** before the first day away when possible. Emergencies are handled case-by-case.

## Steps

1. Check the team calendar for conflicts.
2. Send your manager a written request with dates.
3. After approval, add an out-of-office block on your calendar.
